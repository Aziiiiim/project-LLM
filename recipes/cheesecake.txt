Chantal's New York Cheesecake
This New York cheesecake is rich, creamy, and so delicious. It’s surprisingly easy to make and turns out perfectly every time. Everyone that's tried it has said it tastes just like the ones you'd get in a deli!
Submitted by Chantal Rogers |
Prep Time: 30 mins
Cook Time: 1 hr
Additional Time: 6 hrs
Total Time: 7 hrs 30 mins
Servings: 12
Yield: 1 (9-inch) cheesecake
Directions
Step 1
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
Step 2
Mix graham cracker crumbs and melted butter together in a medium bowl until well combined.
Step 3
Press onto the bottom of the prepared pan to make a packed crust.
Step 4
Mix cream cheese and sugar together in a large bowl until smooth. Blend in milk, then mix in eggs, one at a time, until just combined.
Step 5
Add sour cream, flour, and vanilla; mix until smooth.
Tested by Allrecipes Test Kitchen
Ingredients
15 graham crackers, crushed
2 tablespoons butter, melted
4 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
1 ½ cups white sugar
¾ cup milk
4 large eggs
1 cup sour cream
¼ cup all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Step 6
Pour filling onto prepared crust. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour.
Step 7
Turn the oven off; let cheesecake cool in the oven with the door closed for 5 to 6 hours to prevent cracking. Chill in the refrigerator until serving.
Cook’s Note
This is the perfect make-ahead dessert for holidays or special occasion. I think the flavor and texture benefit from chilling overnight. Cover and refrigerate up to 3 days before serving.
Serve plain for a deli-style cheesecake, or with strawberry or blueberry sauce for a little color and sweetness.
